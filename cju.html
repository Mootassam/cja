September 9 - 12, 2024 | Santa Barbara, California 

Partnership marketing is all about real-life relationships—that’s why networking is at the heart of our annual event. Hosted beachside in sunny Santa Barbara, CJU spans four action-packed days filled with fruitful meetings, networking, learning, and fun. Tap into the magic of connection, collaboration, and energy that comes from 1,000+ marketers coming together to amplify beyond.

We can’t wait to shape the future of performance marketing together at #CJU24!

Create Connections

Whether you want to find new friends or spend time with old ones, meet and mingle in The Zone, Growth Expo, The Beach Party, Cocktail Hour & ½, and more! Creating connections couldn’t be easier with networking events planned throughout the conference, as well as with CJ Universe, our social community and event app. Set up your profile to find other attendees with similar interests, arrange meetings, and schedule your entire agenda—before you even arrive at the conference.

Get Inspired, Learn, & Grow

Between the guest keynote, mainstage sessions, and Discovery Lab trainings, you won’t want to miss our lineup of exhilarating speakers and industry thought leaders. Want to dive deep? Mainstage sessions will inform and educate you on the industry’s most pressing topics. Looking to level up your skills? Pop into a 20-minute training or product demo presented by CJ experts in the Discovery Lab. Content topics will be announced in July.

Slam Dunk Sessions

CJU is all about high-quality content! Get a taste of everything our main stage has to offer by exploring the sessions from last year’s event. From macroeconomic trends to AI to data storytelling—CJU brings experts together to present on the topics you care about most.
