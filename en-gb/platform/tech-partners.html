Tech & Data

Integrated Strategic Tech & Data Partners:

Our innovative, data-driven solutions open the door to unlimited opportunity.

Addingwell

CJ's strategic partner, Addingwell, offers an easy integration solution for CJ advertisers using Google Tag Manager (GTM). Addingwell focuses on providing a seamless server-side tracking solution that enhances accuracy and prevents revenue leaks due to ad blockers and other browser restrictions. Addingwell's server-side solution can be integrated quickly and effortlessly without the need for development resources, streamlining the setup process for CJ advertisers. Additionally, Addingwell's TagHealth functionality allows advertisers to monitor the integrity of CJ tags and key associated events, such as purchases, ensuring optimal performance, and tracking accuracy.

affiliate.ai

CJ's affiliate.ai partnership is designed to enhance efficiency and streamline day to day operations. By integrating affiliate.ai into platforms like Slack or Microsoft Teams, users can access a real-time reporting assistant, allowing more time to devote to programme growth. affiliate.ai is seamlessly integrated with CJ's APIs to ensure you have all the necessary affiliate data when you need it. Whether you’re managing multiple affiliate programmes or just one, affiliate.ai makes affiliate management easier than ever before.

Button

We've partnered with Button to expand our already robust mobile web tracking and in-app tracking capabilities and create a top-notch mobile shopping experience that helps the performance marketing channel evolve. Button empowers the world’s biggest brands to supercharge their mobile marketing revenue and engagement with privacy-first identity, industry-leading deep linking, and intelligent machine learning technology.

Tech partner

CreatorIQ

CJ continues to push the boundaries of tech and expertise to connect brands worldwide with the full spectrum of content and influencer personas by partnering with award-winning enterprise influencer marketing platform, CreatorIQ. Named “the highest performing pure-play in-house influencer platform” by Forrester.

Data partner

Databowl

Run a successful and seamless lead-gen programme with CJ by your side. Databowl delivers unparallelled level of validation on leads—validate every facet of the data clients receive in real time.

Data partner

Epsilon

As part of Publicis Groupe, CJ and Epsilon offer best-in-class outcome-based marketing solutions. With more than 50 years of experience, Epsilon partners with the world’s top brands, agencies, and publishers to drive data-driven growth.

Data partner

Fraudlogix

CJ’s advanced fraud detection technology suite incorporates global web traffic insights from Fraudlogix, which identifies and prevents fraudulent traffic sources including, forged user agents, bot traffic, mismatched geographies, and dozens of other potential indicators of non-compliant activity—designed to keep your programme running safely and smoothly.

Invoca

Powered by Invoca, CJ's Pay Per Call tracking solutions help advertisers connect with prospective customers on the phone where more complex product or service options can be discussed. Invoca’s AI-powered conversation intelligence platform enables clients to deliver seamless buying experiences that drive revenue growth by using the best of digital and human touch.

Tech partner

LiveRamp

Activate your LiveRamp data within CJ. Wherever data lives, LiveRamp’s platform lets you access it, safely transform it into a connected customer view, and activate it at scale with measurable, meaningful outcomes.

Data partner

MikMak

Through MikMak’s partnership with CJ, it’s easier than ever to add affiliate links into display and social ads with full transparency into transactional KPIs to help you understand engagement and performance. MikMak helps consumer product companies—including grocery and CPG brands—understand and perfect their consumers’ online journey, drive online sales at retailer sites, and optimise their media and marketing spend.

Tech partner

Stape

Stape provides the ultimate solution to browser restriction and ad-blocking challenges, offering a server-side tracking solution tailored for clients using Google Tag Manager. With a pricing discount for CJ advertisers, this service can be swiftly set up and deployed, enhancing tracking accuracy by potentially 5-12% and preventing revenue leaks often caused by browser restrictions. The straightforward setup allows clients to implement Stape's tags effortlessly, without the need for any development resources.

Tableau

CJ offers hundreds of customisable data visualisations via Tableau, available on-demand through our Insights platform. Use these visualisations to bring your data to life and drive more intelligent growth.
