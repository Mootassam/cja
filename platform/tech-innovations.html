Pay Per Call

Pay Per Call tracking solutions help advertisers connect with prospective customers on the phone where more complex products or service options can be discussed, leading to higher AOVs and a seamless customer experience. With a focus on call quality, Pay Per Call increases call center sales and expanded advertising reach through both online and offline media channels on a performance basis.

Clickless Tracking

CJ's Clickless Tracking solution allows advertisers to assign exclusive promo codes to publisher promotional properties. Once an exclusive code is assigned to a publisher, they will always receive credit for any transaction when the code is used – even without a click. Publishers will be able to promote their exclusive code wherever they reach their audience - online, radio & podcasts, YouTube, Social Media channels, print publications, and beyond.

04

Optimize

Insights on demand, made easy.

Understand how your new, lapsed, and returning customers interact with your partners, view the customer journey touchpoints that drive high-value outcomes, understand affiliate incrementality, and analyze how you stack up against your competitors through our comprehensive benchmarking suite.  

Affiliate Incrementality

Digital marketing today includes a wide range of channels that are vying for investment. With a myriad of ways for consumers to connect with brands, the bar has been set higher than ever for channels to prove their value. For brands to intelligently invest, it is important for them to truly understand the value each channel delivers, and CJ is uniquely poised to deliver this analysis.​

Read More
Affiliate Customer Journey

Explore the influence of your CJ partnerships across the entire customer purchase journey—from discovery, through evaluation, to decision. This dashboard will help you understand which publishers enter the purchase journey early, which are trusted contributors to product research, and which help close the deal in the decision stage. View revenue, AOV, as well as total transactions per journey type and by publisher, enabling you to measure successful paths by several key metrics beyond conversions.​​

Read More
Competitive Benchmarking Suite

Verticalized benchmarking across retail, travel, finance, and network service sectors allows you to leverage share-of-voice and share-of-wallet insight to understand where you fall within the competitive landscape. Competitive Publisher Indexing lets you monitor the relative effectiveness of your publisher relationships, compared to similar advertisers.

Program Performance Insights & Customer Status Analysis

Intuitively designed and customizable period-over-period program analysis allows you to understand your program performance and measure success by the metrics that matter most: partner, revenue, ROAS, device and browser type, product category, audience location, coupon/promo code, Situational Commissioning scenarios, and more.​ Evaluate how your new, lapsed, and returning consumers interact with your partners to empower you to drive smarter customer segment strategies, including the percent of affiliate transactions driven by new, lapsed, and return customer segments, and AOV and revenue by customer segment at the partner level.

Read More
Shopper Loyalty & Market Share Analysis

Gain insight into the revenue that your brand's shoppers or customers are spending with competing categories and brands. This can suggest that shoppers are making purchases between brands, and there’s an opportunity to push offers to retain loyalty and acquire new shoppers. Leverage a data-driven approach to measure the effectiveness of your offers and placements within your competitive set.
